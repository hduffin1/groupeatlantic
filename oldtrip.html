2019 Trip

In May 2019, we took our most loyal installers to Miami in the sunshine state of Florida.



The world famous FountainBleau hotel on Miami beach played host on the trip. The five star luxury property is the setting for countless movies, pop videos and premieres. Activities included a helicopter and airboat experience out in the Everglades, Golf at the Normandy Golf Course on Miami Beach, a private pool party at the hotel, dining at top restaurants alongside a fantastic finale to say ‘Thank You’!

The positive feedback from the trip has been overwhelming and the amazing experience our installers had can been seen in some photos and video from the trip below.
